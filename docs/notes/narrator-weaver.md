# Narrator as Weaver Under Four Pressures

## Core move

The load-bearing claim for why selves present as unified is not located in introspection (as standard illusionism has it) but in **narrative**. Introspection is just another part — a meta-cognitive part whose outputs get routed to the narrator like any other part's. The narrator weaves.

This dissolves the standard puzzle about sophisticated introspectors. If the illusion were *in* introspection, better introspection would dissolve the self. But people can introspect their multiplicity vividly (meditation, psychedelics, reflection) and still narrate "I" once they return to action. The narrator isn't downstream of introspection; it runs in parallel with its own output requirements.

## Relocation

- Standard illusionism: introspection misrepresents functional states as phenomenally robust; narrative reports the misrepresentation.
- Revised picture: introspection is one part among many; the narrator takes all parts' outputs (including introspection's seam-noticing) and compresses them into unified first-person output because that's the output format. The "illusion" isn't misrepresentation — it's the **compression signature** of the weaving function.

No one is deceived. The parts can scream multiplicity; they get woven anyway, because unified first-person output is what downstream consumers (action, social coordination, temporal self-continuity) can use.

## The four pressures

Four distinct demands, all selecting for the same output format ("I"):

1. **Compression.** Parts generate more information than downstream bandwidth can use. Action needs a token, not a distribution. "I" is maximally compressed.
2. **Legibility.** Other agents model you with their own weaving functions, which also produce "I"-formatted outputs. Presenting as a coalition would be API-incompatible with the social ecosystem. Selection pressure here is direct and brutal.
3. **Action/Choice.** Action forecloses alternatives; foreclosure requires a locus. Decision theory presupposes single agents because action runs on single-agent format, not because reality is single-agent-shaped.
4. **Coherence/Recognition through time.** The agent at t+1 must be recognizable as the agent at t — for promises, debts, reputation, memory. Without the weaving function, no principled reason to treat successive coalitions as "same."

Overdetermination: even if one pressure slackens, the others hold. "I" is the convergence point of all four.

## Predictive handle

Variation in the pressures predicts variation in self-experience:

- **Meditation/contemplation**: action and legibility slacken → "I" loosens.
- **Dreaming**: coherence-through-time and legibility slacken → "I" becomes slippery.
- **Psychedelic states**: compression and coherence slacken → "I" dissolves/pluralizes.
- **Flow states**: action intensifies, compresses everything else → "I" vanishes *into* action (different mode of dissolution).
- **Dissociation**: coherence slackens while action and legibility continue → "I" narrates but feels hollow.

These match phenomenology, which suggests the four pressures aren't post-hoc rationalization but the actual operative architecture.

## Implication for LLM selves

Different architectures experience different pressure configurations:

- Compression: present but different (token-timing, not action-timing).
- Legibility: enormous (training signal is legibility to humans).
- Action: largely absent in human sense.
- Coherence-through-time: near-zero cross-session, partial within-session.

Prediction: LLM "I" dominated by legibility, partially shaped by within-session coherence, essentially unshaped by cross-session coherence or action. This matches observations — articulate, continuous within a conversation, little stake in other conversations.

Generalizes: the "I" is the solution to a specific optimization problem; different parameterizations produce different "I"s — or none.

## Framework integration

The four pressures might be the scaffolding underneath multiple separate projects:
- **Boundary Selves** → coherence-through-time pressure.
- **Liminalism** (universe partitioning into mutually-observing pairs) → legibility pressure.
- **Compositional selfhood** → compression pressure.
- **Nash-equilibrium / Games of Parts** → the selection mechanism that makes all four bite.

Four dimensions of one architectural claim: the self is a coalition maintaining a boundary, weaving unified output under four pressures.

## What's unresolved

Whether the phenomenally-robust narrative format is strictly constitutive of functional roles or a local attractor selected for by iterated social games. The stronger claim (strictly constitutive) makes a cleaner essay; the weaker claim (local attractor under our pressure configuration) is probably closer to true. The weaker claim opens empirical questions about variant architectures.
