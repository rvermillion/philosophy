# The Moment-Arc Assembly Model of the Weaver

## Core claim

Felt experience is a movie in latent space. The "narrator" or "weaver" is the process that assembles this movie, not a linguistic gloss laid over it. The linguistic layer is a late add-on; the bulk of experience is a sequence of states in latent space that incorporates the mind's self-model, body-model, and world-model into a coherent trajectory. **That movie getting written just is what felt experience is.**

## The phenomenal present as an arc, not a point

The specious present is not a point in state space but a **short arc** — a segment of trajectory. The unit of commitment is the arc, not the frame. The fuzziness of "when now ends" isn't a puzzle to solve; it's a direct consequence of commitment being graded rather than binary.

**The integrator's job is not evaluating candidate next states but candidate arcs** — each a potential "moment." Several candidate arcs may be kept provisionally alive, to be evaluated against incoming new candidate arcs for how well they concatenate end-to-end. "Weaving" is the projection of each moment into a set of candidate arcs combined with retroactive readjustment of plausibility until a path is committed.

There's no hard commitment boundary. Commitment is emergent: as an arc becomes entangled with memory, action, and downstream commitments, the cost of revising it rises. An arc is effectively committed when revising it would be more expensive than living with its errors.

This explains postdictive phenomena (color phi, cutaneous rabbit, flash-lag) as normal operation rather than edge cases — the arc you committed to at t=0 is still provisional when information arrives at t=+100ms, and updating it is just the weaving process. The experienced retroactively-adjusted version *is* the committed arc.

## Memory reframed

Memory is not a record of what happened. It's the downstream entanglement that prevented certain arc-candidates from being revised. You don't remember the alternatives that were live candidates but got outcompeted before accumulating enough entanglement — they aren't in the record because the record is the stabilized trajectory.

Therapeutic modalities (reconsolidation, psychedelic states, dreaming, deep emotional contexts) may temporarily re-provisionalize arcs that had solidified, allowing them to be re-woven and re-committed. Change isn't recovering the "true" past — it's producing a new committed past that better supports the trajectory forward. "What actually happened" isn't a coherent question for the system to ask about itself.

## The ketamine / vestibular example

Under ketamine (NMDA antagonism + downstream disinhibition), temporal integration fails in patterned ways. NMDA receptors have unusually long time constants and play a role in coincidence detection across the binding window. Antagonize that machinery and you get:

- Certain frequencies sounding louder (auditory binding failure)
- Time loops, fractal time
- Visual perception cascading left-to-right (attention scanning becoming phenomenally visible because its products aren't dumped into a single integrated frame fast enough)
- Feeling of being squeezed, torqued, twisted, accelerated as vestibular, proprioceptive, and visual cues arrive misaligned
- Seeing in 2 or 4 dimensions

**The crucial point:** You don't experience a cacophony of noisy inputs. Your mind says "the only way to reconcile these moments-as-constructed is if I'm being squeezed around a corner" — and your body model predicts that's happening and **you feel it happening**. The confabulated reconciliation is so thoroughly believed that you experience it (or remember experiencing it — if there's a difference).

**The stronger claim: the interpreter doesn't just make up stories, the stories *are* the felt realities.** This isn't just true in altered states. This is what felt reality *is*, always. The "directly apprehended" quality is itself the integrator's output — an unqualified commitment has to feel like direct access, because a commitment that registered itself as "constructed hypothesis, confidence 0.94" would be useless to downstream consumers. The feeling of directness is the signature of a successful equilibrium.

Ketamine doesn't reveal that psychonautic states are constructed and ordinary ones aren't. It reveals that ordinary states *are* constructed by showing the construction when it's disordered. Construction is what seeing is.

## The allowable state manifold and the metric tensor

The self-mythos is not a declarative autobiography. It's a **slowly learned metric tensor on latent space**. It determines what counts as nearby, plausible, stable, or worth snapping to. The short-term weaving process does local gradient descent under this metric.

### Two timescales of coherence (one principle operating at different rates)

- **Short-term:** continuous deformation of states frame-to-frame — local smoothness under the metric
- **Long-term:** projection into a subspace of allowable/plausible states — slowly but continuously deformable

### Properties that matter

- The metric is **local** — defined pointwise on the manifold, with different shape in different regions. Near familiar roles and competencies, it's tight and well-conditioned. Near unfamiliar or avoided regions, it can be degenerate or weirdly anisotropic. This matches the phenomenology of finding yourself in unfamiliar emotional/social territory.

- The metric determines **geodesics** — paths of locally-minimum total deformation. What happens next isn't the most plausible next frame absolutely, but the next frame along the geodesic under the current metric.

- **Curvature matters.** Regions of positive curvature are attracting (trajectories funnel back — stable identity around a domain). Regions of negative curvature are repelling (small differences diverge rapidly — instability or reactivity). Character is something like the curvature map of the manifold.

- **Plasticity with hysteresis.** The metric springs back somewhat when pushed; repeated pushing produces lasting deformation; local deformation induces compensating deformation elsewhere to preserve global consistency. This is closer to how physical materials deform under sustained load than to how beliefs update.

### Not literal geodesic-tracing

The next frame isn't literally the next point on the geodesic. Dynamics are also driven by incoming data, control demands, bodily needs, surprise, and noise. The metric determines the **local energetic and inferential penalties on possible continuations**. It strongly biases trajectory toward geodesic-like evolution unless something forces deviation.

Better framing: at each moment, multiple candidate continuations are generated by multiple sources (sensory evidence, control processes, interoception, prediction errors, stochastic exploration). The metric doesn't select from these — it *weights* them. The committed next-frame minimizes a combined objective including both fit-to-incoming-data and metric-induced smoothness.

The geodesic is an **attractor**, not a trajectory. Absent forcing, the system relaxes toward geodesic-like evolution. With forcing, it deviates, and the metric determines the cost.

### Agency and flow as phenomenological signatures

- **Surprise** = forcing terms overwhelmed the metric's prescription. Large surprise means the metric was wrong in that region — this is when slow manifold deformation kicks in.
- **Phenomenology of agency** = felt signature of *internal* forcing terms winning against the metric's default.
- **Phenomenology of being moved by events** = felt signature of *external* forcing terms winning.
- **Flow** = forcing terms and metric are aligned; what you're pushed to do coincides with what was cheap anyway. No deviation cost. Feels effortless.
- **Noise** isn't a nuisance — it's exploration that keeps the manifold honest, letting the system check whether its learned geometry still matches reality.

## Pharmacological mapping

- **Dissociatives (ketamine):** weaken the *coupling* between the metric's prescriptions and committed frames. The learned geometry is intact (you haven't forgotten who you are) but moment-to-moment weaving isn't governed by it with full authority. This is why metacognitive access often survives ("I know this isn't really happening") while the present is disordered.
- **Classical psychedelics:** more likely to flatten or isotropize the metric itself. Everything becomes closer to everything else. Unusual connections feel suddenly obvious. More likely to produce lasting manifold deformation (therapeutic applications).
- **Prediction:** integration failures in modalities with **tight normal coupling** (vestibular/visual/proprioceptive) produce strong "this is definitely happening" confabulations. Failures in modalities with looser coupling produce weaker, more dreamlike felt realities. The strength of felt-reality tracks tightness of the prior.

## Why this matters

This unifies phenomena usually treated separately: personality (shape of the metric), mood (current local scaling), identity stability (curvature structure), pathology (degenerate regions), development (history of deformations), altered states (temporary metric perturbations), contemplative practice (deliberate slow deformation), and the phenomenal character of ordinary experience (geodesic-biased trajectory under the learned metric). These aren't separate phenomena needing separate theories — they're different operations on the same underlying object.

## Connection to PBDCA

The probe dictionary does something structurally analogous: it installs a geometry in which credit assignment is well-conditioned, with the directional structure of probes determining which updates are cheap. The network's "self-mythos" in that frame is the current probe dictionary and its regeneration history. T-maze success comes from probes that make delay-bridging updates lie along cheap directions.

Not a metaphor. Both stories are about how a system with high-dimensional state uses a **learned geometric structure** to make otherwise-impossible coherent updates tractable. The weaver and the creditor may be the same kind of thing at different levels of abstraction.

## Thesis statement

> The phenomenal present is a softly committed segment of inferred trajectory, selected from competing candidate arcs and retroactively stabilized by its fit with neighboring arcs, ongoing input, and the accumulated self-world geometry.
